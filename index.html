Usage Guidelines

The RF emblem represents focus and precision. Use the circular mark for social media, app icons, and compact applications. The full wordmark is preferred for marketing materials, website headers, and official communications.

Color Strategy

Navy conveys authority and financial confidence. Gold adds warmth and premium positioning—appropriate for the senior audience. Limit gold usage to 10% or less for maximum impact. The palette moves away from generic corporate blue toward a distinctive, ownable system.
